You can calculate either:

Cgpa and GPA or

GPA only.

To calculate your cgpa and gpa

  1. Enter the total number of points you have gotten so far (ie the largest number you see on your result)
  2. Enter the total number of units you have done (ie before the semester you want to calculate)
  3. Enter the number of course you want to calculate your present GPA for(this will determine the number of rows to show)
  4. click on to create the table

To calculate your gpa

  1. course column is optional (but can be filled for reference purpose)
  2. fill the units in each column(*compulsory and accurately)
  3. Fill the respective score for each Unit(*compulsory and accurately)
  4. click on (this will both calculate your cgpa and gpa)
  5. (YOU CAN CALCULATE ONLY YOUR GPA BY SKIPPING INSTRUCTION 1 AND 2 )
