      WHY JETARchive

      We went through a lot of pain while trying to get quality and authentic materials for the different courses on campus and we know that most students experience this too.
      As students, most times we end up failing a test or exam simply because of materials and information we never had but a friend, colleague or senior was in possession of.

      This platform was created so as to enable students help each other out, because Students and even staffs can easily make available any resourse such as question papers, books, handouts, links, videos and any form of material to be easily accessible to all students.
      You can download and upload these materials on any course/subject.
