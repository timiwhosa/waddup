Futa post utme usually comes up around july and it cost around N2,000 to purchase the form and around N3000 or less, for past question.

      Now truth be told, Futa post utme is not hard, it has never been hard and even futa is not about being tough/hard. Futa is mainly about your ability to think, and solve problems fast

      Going straight to the point,

      Date: currently not fixed but usually july,

      Cost: total N5000 (N2000 for registration and N3000 for past-questions).


      Futa Post-Utme Requirements:

      Subject combination : check out our blog post on that here.

      O'level requirement: minimum of five(5) credit passes including english and mathematics

      Jamb score: 180 and above.

      Accept Direct Entry: Yes.

      External exams: Waec,Neco, Jupeb, IJMB, Poly/ND

      Futa Post-Utme Exam

      Date: Not fixed yet, but based on previous years, it comes up around JULY

      Duration: 4 days , more details below

      Number of subjects: 5 (your jamb combination and general paper).

      Exam length: 30min (practise now on our cbt platform for free)

      Number of questions: 5 questions from each subject, total of 25 questions.

      Mark per question: 4marks (ie, each question is 4marks.... now you know, read o!!)

      Score: over 100 ie 25 x 4marks

      % of total: 50% (your post utme accounts for 50% of your total point/ cut off)

      How to register:

      - Visit www.futa.edu.ng

      - Login with your utme number,

      - Generate RRR and make payment using REMITA (don't pay on any other platform o)

      - Confirm your payment on futa official website,

      - Finally, fill the Post-Utme form online and Print out copies for documentation.

      also, your are required to upload your O,LEVEL result before the speculated deadline and the deadline will not be extended.
