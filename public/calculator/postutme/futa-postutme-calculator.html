Futa Post-Utme Cutt off mark Calculator

      Futa Post-Utme is conducted every year with at least 15 to 20 thousand applicants, and only 3,500 (less than 4,000) students are admitted. ie 17.5% are admitted. Surprised? of course you should be. But the question is What must i do to get admitted.

      Well, I have a high jamb score, am good to go right? not really. But you should know that your admission depends on several factors:

      • Department you are appying to (how competitive is it.)
      • Your Jamb score,
      • Post-Utme score
      • How many people applying to that Department have a high jamb and post utme score. To know more about this, check our blog post on How is Futa departmental cut off mark gotten or calculated.
      • Your SSCE result (Number of sitting and how strong it is)
      • for DE, what's your A-Level score
        • Did you do your A-Level in futa,
        • Did you do it somewhere else.
      for more details about all this, read our blog post on Futa post utme, and 10 things to do to pass futa Post-Utme.

      How this Calculator works

      Firstly, You should know that the minimum you must score in your post utme irrespective of your jamb score, is 60%. Hence, the lowest score that this calculator shows is 60%.
